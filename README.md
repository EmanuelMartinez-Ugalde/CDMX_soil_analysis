# CDMX_soil_analysis
This repository contains the scripts used to perform diversity analysis, network inference, and figures for the paper "Microbial community networks reveal ecological consequences of soil management in Mexico City conservation landscape"

These shape files were used in the section 6 of the "Martínez-Ugalde et al. 2026 - Diversity and Network Analysis" notebook and correspond to 
suelo-de-conservacion.shp = polygon of the conservation soil area of Mexico City
dest_2010cw.shp = polygon of Mexico City
9as.shp = polygon of the human settlement in Mexico City
